## Design Style

Style: Modern, sleek, clean, subtle depths. Think subtle shadows, frosted glass effects, and glowing accents.

Layout:

Desktop: Fixed left side navigation bar (approx. 20-25% width). Sections scroll vertically or horizontally (your preference) in the main content area (75-80% width).

Mobile: Navigation bar shifts to the top (fixed or hidden on scroll). Sections stack vertically.

Navigation: Clear icons and short text labels for sections. Clicking or tapping a menu item triggers a smooth animated transition to that section.

Transitions: Implement "scroll snapping" or a "fade-and-slide" transition between sections. The background could subtly change or have animated elements during the transition.

Color Palette: Dominate with soft neutrals (light grays, blues) for the background. Use brighter accent colors (teals, greens, purples) for hover states, active menu items, and subtle visual elements.

Content: Use clear headings, organized card layouts for projects and skills, and legible body text. Include subtle coding accents or terminal-like snippets to highlight devops skills.

Keywords: Neumorphism, Glassmorphism, modern, clean, smooth, interactive, subtle, glow, transitions.
